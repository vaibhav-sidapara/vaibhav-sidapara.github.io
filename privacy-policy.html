Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all projects developed and published by us.

  • No Personal Data Collection: Our apps/extensions do not collect, store, or share personal information, browsing history, or user data outside of what is required for functionality.
  • Use of Google User Data: Some apps/extensions (such as our bookmark backup tool) use Google OAuth to authenticate users and access their Google Drive. We only request the minimum scopes necessary to create, read, and update backup files in the user’s Google Drive. No other Google data (such as Gmail, Contacts, or Calendar) is accessed. This access is used only to enable bookmark backup and restore functionality.
  • Local Usage & Token Storage: The OAuth access token is stored securely on the user’s device using Chrome’s storage.local. The extension does not transmit this token or any Google data to external servers. All communication with Google APIs is encrypted via HTTPS.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, share, or transmit your Google account data or bookmarks to any third parties.
  • Data Protection: Since all operations occur locally in your browser or within your own Google Drive, your information remains private and secure. Users may revoke Google Drive access at any time from their Google Account permissions page.
  • Data Retention & Deletion: Backup files are stored in your Google Drive under a folder created by the extension. You may delete these files manually at any time. If you uninstall the extension, locally stored tokens are removed automatically.
  • Permissions: Requested permissions (e.g., access to bookmarks, identity, and storage) are used solely to provide the core features of the app/extension.
  • Security: We implement security measures by limiting requested scopes to the minimum required and ensuring all communication with Google services is encrypted.

By using our apps and extensions, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you have questions or concerns, please contact us at vaibhav(dot)sidapara(at)gmail.com.
